合併成功後，您就可以用同一個信箱，無論選擇「Email 帳號」還是「社群帳號」登入，都能順利進入同一個帳號。

歡迎前往「[會員檔案](https://www.macromicro.me/user/settings)」確認目前登入方式，若登入後發現訂閱權益或設定沒有同步，請來信 support@macromicro.me 告訴我們，將由客服團隊為您確認。

<!--locale:zh-cn-->

合并成功后，您就可以用同一个信箱，无论选择「Email 帐号」还是「社群帐号」登入，都能顺利进入同一个帐号。

欢迎前往「[会员档案](https://sc.macromicro.me/user/settings)」确认目前登入方式，若登入后发现订阅权益或设定没有同步，请来信 support@macromicro.me 告诉我们，将由客服团队为您确认。

<!--locale:en-->

Once the merge is complete, you can use the same email address to log in — whether through Email Account or Social Account — and access the same account.

You can visit your [Member Profile](https://en.macromicro.me/user/settings) to confirm your current login methods. If you notice that your subscription benefits or settings aren't synced after logging in, please contact support@macromicro.me and we'll look into it for you.
