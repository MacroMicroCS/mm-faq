在尚未登入時，系統將會引導先登入。於登入成功，點擊畫面右上角頭像即可開啟選單，進行與會員帳號相關的一系列設定。

![未命名設計.gif](https://support.macromicro.me/hc/article_attachments/15554709020943)

<!--locale:zh-cn-->

在尚未登入时，系统将会引导先登入。于登入成功，点击画面右上角头像即可开启选单，进行与会员帐号相关的一系列设定。

![未命名設計 (2).gif](https://support.macromicro.me/hc/article_attachments/15554760058895)

<!--locale:en-->

Before logging in, the system will guide you to log in first. After a successful login, click the avatar at the top right corner of the screen to open the menu and perform a series of settings related to the member account.

![未命名設計 (1).gif](https://support.macromicro.me/hc/article_attachments/15554788491535)
