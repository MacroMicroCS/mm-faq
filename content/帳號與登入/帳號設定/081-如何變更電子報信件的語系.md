目前變更電子報信件的語系需要由客服人工協助，操作很簡單，來信告訴我們就好！

請來信 support@macromicro.me，並告知：
・會員信箱
・希望改為的語系（中文 / 英文）

客服確認後將於工作時間（週一至週五 10:00–18:00 GMT+8）為您完成設定。若有任何問題，歡迎隨時聯繫我們！

<!--locale:en-->

Changing the newsletter language currently requires manual assistance from our support team — just drop us an email!

Please contact support@macromicro.me and include:
・Your registered email address
・Your preferred language (Chinese / English)

Our team will update your settings during business hours (Mon–Fri, 10:00–18:00 GMT+8). Feel free to reach out if you have any questions!
