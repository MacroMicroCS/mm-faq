請於登入訂閱帳號後，點擊網站右上角的 **會員頭像選單** 前往 [會員檔案](https://www.macromicro.me/user/settings) > [付費紀錄](https://www.macromicro.me/user/order-history) 查詢。

<!--locale:zh-cn-->

请于登入订阅帐号后，点击网站右上角的 **会员头像选单** 前往 [会员档案](https://sc.macromicro.me/user/settings) > [付费纪录](https://sc.macromicro.me/user/order-history) 查询。

<!--locale:en-->

To view your payment history, click on the profile picture in the top right corner, go to [Member Profile](https://en.macromicro.me/user/settings) and click on [Payment History](https://en.macromicro.me/user/order-history).
