查看付費紀錄很簡單：

登入訂閱帳號後，點擊網站右上角的「會員頭像選單」→「[會員檔案](https://www.macromicro.me/user/settings)」→「付費紀錄」，即可查詢所有付款與訂閱記錄。

<!--locale:zh-cn-->

查看付费记录很简单：

登录订阅账号后，点击网站右上角的「会员头像菜单」→「[会员档案](https://sc.macromicro.me/user/settings)」→「付费记录」，即可查询所有付款与订阅记录。

<!--locale:en-->

Finding your payment history is easy:

After logging in, click the member avatar menu in the upper-right corner →「Member Profile」→「Payment History」to view all your payment and subscription records.
