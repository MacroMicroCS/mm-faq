[訂閱](https://www.macromicro.me/subscribe#individual)財經Ｍ平方的服務目前提供以下幾種付款方式：

[Macromicro Subscription](https://en.macromicro.me/subscribe#individual) currently offers the following payment methods:

\n- Credit Card: Visa, Master, JCB, billed in TWD. American Express and debit card payments are not accepted
\n- UnionPay Card: only for one-time quarterly or annual payment plans
\n- PayPal: only for non-Taiwan users, billed in USD
