請放心，您的信用卡資訊受到嚴密保護！

- 所有信用卡相關資訊皆以 SSL 256bit 加密機制保管於金流系統，MacroMicro 平台本身不儲存完整卡號

- 平台僅記錄信用卡的前六碼與後四碼，用於對帳確認，就像您刷卡後收到的簽單資訊一樣。

Rest assured — your credit card information is thoroughly protected.

・All card-related data is stored in the payment system using SSL 256-bit encryption. MacroMicro does not store your full card number.
・Only the first 6 and last 4 digits are recorded for billing verification purposes — the same information you'd see on a transaction receipt.
