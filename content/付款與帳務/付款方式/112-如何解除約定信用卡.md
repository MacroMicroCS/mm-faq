如果僅是希望暫時先取消訂閱，操作方式如下：
### 信用卡付款訂閱
前往「會員檔案」→「付費紀錄」，調整訂閱狀態即可。建議在扣款日前完成操作。

### PayPal 訂閱
請回到 PayPal 平台自行取消綁定。若操作遇到困難，也歡迎來信 support@macromicro.me，我們可以協助您處理。"

若您需要解除約定信用卡，請以會員信箱來信 support@macromicro.me。

⚠️ 解除前請確認：若訂閱仍在效期內，解除信用卡後將無法自動續約。
💡 若只是想換一張扣款信用卡（不取消訂閱）：請前往「[會員檔案](https://www.macromicro.me/user/settings)」>「[付費紀錄](https://www.macromicro.me/user/order-history)」>「更換/設定信用卡」自行操作即可，不需聯繫客服！

If you just want to pause or cancel your subscription, here's how:
### Credit card subscription
Go to [Member Profile](https://www.macromicro.me/user/settings) → [Payment History](https://www.macromicro.me/user/order-history) and adjust your subscription status. Please do this before your next billing date.

### PayPal subscription
Cancel the recurring payment in your PayPal account. If you need help, email support@macromicro.me.

To remove a linked credit card entirely, email support@macromicro.me from your registered email address.

⚠️ Before removing: If your subscription is still active, removing the card will prevent auto-renewal.
💡 If you just want to switch to a different card (without cancelling your subscription): Go to [Member Profile](https://www.macromicro.me/user/settings) > [Payment History](https://www.macromicro.me/user/order-history) >「Update Payment Info」
