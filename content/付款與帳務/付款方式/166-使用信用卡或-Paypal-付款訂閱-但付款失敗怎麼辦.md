遇到付款問題真的很困擾，我們來幫您排查看看！以下是常見原因與解決方式：\
\
1. 網路問題：請更換網路環境（例如切換至手機行動網路）後再試一次\
2. 企業防火牆：若在公司網路下操作，防火牆可能阻擋金流連線，建議改用個人網路\
3. 3D OTP 驗證未完成：確認是否已收到銀行簡訊驗證碼，未完成驗證將無法完成扣款\
4. 信用卡設定：確認信用卡未被銀行限制海外交易，可致電發卡銀行確認\
\
若以上方式仍無法解決，請來信 [support@macromicro.me](mailto:support@macromicro.me%E3%80%82) 並附上錯誤截圖，我們會進一步協助您！

4. 信用卡设定：确认信用卡未被银行限制海外交易，可致电发卡银行确认\

Payment issues can be frustrating — let's troubleshoot together! Here are the most common causes and solutions:\
\
1. Network issue: Try switching to a different network (e.g., mobile data) and retry\
2. Corporate firewall: If you're on a company network, the firewall may be blocking the payment gateway — try using a personal network\
3. 3D OTP verification incomplete: Check whether you received the bank's SMS verification code — payment cannot go through without completing verification\
4. Credit card settings: Verify that your card isn't restricted for international transactions by contacting your card issuer\
\
If none of the above resolves the issue, email support@macromicro.me with a screenshot of the error and we'll investigate further.
