更改統編很簡單，自己就可以操作！

登入後前往[會員檔案](https://www.macromicro.me/user/settings)→「編輯」→「發票設定」，即可更改統編相關資訊。

修改後的統編將從下一次付款起套用。

<!--locale:zh-cn-->

更改统编很简单，自己就可以操作！

登录后前往[会员档案](https://sc.macromicro.me/user/settings)→「编辑」→「发票设置」，即可更改统编相关信息。

修改后的统编将从下一次付款起套用。

<!--locale:en-->

Updating your tax ID is easy — you can do it yourself!

After logging in, [go to Member Profile](https://en.macromicro.me/user/settings) →「Edit」→「Invoice Settings」and update your tax ID information.

Changes will apply starting from your next payment.
