M幣是財經M平方的虛擬貨幣，可以用來兌換 M平方服務與商品！

目前 1 枚 M幣 相當於 20 元新台幣，可以兌換：
・總經線上學院的課程
・MM研究員撰寫的投資月報
・MM Prime 訂閱效期

獲取方式有兩種：
1. 透過信用卡直接[購買M幣](https://www.macromicro.me/user/mcoins/checkout)
2. 參加[用戶觀點](https://www.macromicro.me/shared_chart)投票或[透過邀請碼推薦好友訂閱](https://www.macromicro.me/user/settings)，免費獲得 M幣

前往「[我的M幣](https://www.macromicro.me/user/mcoins)」頁面 即可查看目前持有數量和兌換選項

M-Coins are MacroMicro's virtual currency, redeemable for services and products on the platform!

1 M-Coin is equivalent to NT$20 and can be used for:
・Online Academy courses
・MM monthly investment reports
・MM Prime subscription time

There are two ways to earn M-Coins:
1. [purchase M-coins](https://www.macromicro.me/user/mcoins/checkout) directly with a credit card
2. Earn for free by participating in [User Insights](https://en.macromicro.me/shared_chart) sentiment votes or r[eferring friends to subscribe to MacroMicro](https://en.macromicro.me/user/settings)

Visit [My M-Coins](https://en.macromicro.me/user/mcoins) to check your balance and explore redemption options.
