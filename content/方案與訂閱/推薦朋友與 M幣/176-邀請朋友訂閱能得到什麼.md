邀請朋友一起加入，雙方都有好康！\
\
使用您的專屬「推薦碼」推薦好友訂閱 MM 會員，好友訂閱成功後，您和好友都可以獲得 [M幣](https://www.macromicro.me/user/mcoins) 作為回饋！\
\
推薦碼可在「[會員檔案](https://www.macromicro.me/user/settings)」中找到，快去分享給朋友吧！

邀请朋友一起加入，双方都有好康！\
\
使用您的专属「推荐码」推荐好友订阅 MM 会员，好友订阅成功后，您和好友都可以获得 [M币](https://sc.macromicro.me/user/mcoins) 作为回馈！\
\
推荐码可在「[会员档案](https://sc.macromicro.me/user/settings)」中找到，快去分享给朋友吧！

<!--locale:en-->

Refer a friend and both of you benefit!\
\
Share your personal referral code with a friend — once they subscribe to a MM plan, both of you will earn [M-Coins](https://en.macromicro.me/user/mcoins) as a reward!\
\
Find your referral code in your [Member Profile](https://en.macromicro.me/user/settings). Start sharing!
