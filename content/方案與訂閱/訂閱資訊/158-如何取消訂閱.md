感謝您一直以來的使用與支持！若有取消訂閱的需求，以下是操作方式：

### 信用卡付款訂閱
可自行於扣款日前前往 [會員檔案](https://www.macromicro.me/user/settings) > [付費紀錄](https://www.macromicro.me/user/order-history)，調整訂閱狀態為取消。

### PayPal 訂閱
通常可在 PayPal 平台自行取消綁定。若操作中遇到問題，歡迎來信 support@macromicro.me，我們會協助您處理。

取消後，自下期起停止自動續約，目前效期內的使用權益不受影響，您仍可正常使用至效期結束。

📌 溫馨提醒：持續訂閱可終身保有加入時的優惠方案；若取消後重新訂閱，將適用當時最新價格，請列入考量。

若有任何疑問，歡迎隨時來信 support@macromicro.me，我們很樂意協助！

Thank you for your continued support! Here's how to cancel:

### Credit card subscription
Go to [Member Profile](https://en.macromicro.me/user/settings) > [Payment History](https://en.macromicro.me/user/order-history) before your next billing date and change your subscription status to cancelled.

### PayPal subscription
You can usually cancel the recurring payment directly in your PayPal account. If you run into issues, email support@macromicro.me and we'll help.

After cancellation, auto-renewal stops from the next billing cycle. Your current access remains unaffected until the end of your paid period.

📌 Reminder: Continuous subscribers keep their original price permanently. If you cancel and re-subscribe later, current pricing will apply.

If you have any questions, feel free to email support@macromicro.me — we're happy to help!
