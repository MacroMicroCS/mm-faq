  en: Can I still view reports I received during my subscription after cancelling?

請放心，您訂閱期間獲得的報告不會消失！

所有報告會統一收納在「[MM獨家報告](https://www.macromicro.me/mails/monthly_report)」中，即使停止訂閱後仍然可以持續觀看，這是我們給每位曾經訂閱會員的保留權益。

Don't worry — reports you received during your subscription won't disappear!

[All reports are stored in the MM Exclusive Reports section](https://en.macromicro.me/mails/monthly_report) and remain accessible even after your subscription ends. This is a benefit we preserve for all past subscribers.
