原則上，訂閱於啟用後即開始使用，故無法提供已使用期間的退款，敬請見諒。
您可以隨時取消自動續訂，取消後，自下期起停止自動續約，目前效期內的使用權益不受影響，您仍可正常使用至效期結束。

取消訂閱操作方式如下：

### 信用卡付款訂閱

前往 [會員檔案](https://www.macromicro.me/user/settings) > [付費紀錄](https://www.macromicro.me/user/order-history)，調整訂閱狀態即可。建議在扣款日前完成操作。

### PayPal 訂閱

請回到 PayPal 平台自行取消綁定。若操作遇到困難，也歡迎來信 support@macromicro.me，我們可以協助您處理。

As a general policy, subscriptions begin upon activation and refunds for used time are not provided — we appreciate your understanding.

You can cancel auto-renewal at any time. Once cancelled, auto-renewal stops from the next cycle, and your current access remains unaffected until the end of your paid period.

To cancel, follow these steps:

### Credit card subscription

Go to [Member Profile](https://en.macromicro.me/user/settings) > [Payment History](https://en.macromicro.me/user/order-history) and adjust your subscription status. Please do this before your next billing date.

### PayPal subscription

Cancel the recurring payment directly in your PayPal account. If you run into issues, email support@macromicro.me — we can help！
