訂閱後可以看到的獨家報告說明如下：

成為訂閱會員後，您將可以從 [MM 獨家報告](https://www.macromicro.me/mails/monthly_report) 立即觀看以下內容：

1. 最新一篇投資月報

2. 最近期的 5 篇獨家快報

此外，也可以前往 [部落格](https://www.macromicro.me/blog) ，瀏覽過往訂閱會員限定的獨家報告，讓您回顧過去的市場分析與研究員觀點。

订阅后可以看到的独家报告说明如下：

成为订阅会员后，您将可以从 [MM 独家报告](https://sc.macromicro.me/mails/monthly_report) 立即观看以下内容：

1. 最新一篇投资月报

2. 最近期的 5 篇独家快报

此外，也可以前往 [部落格](https://sc.macromicro.me/blog) ，浏览过往订阅会员限定的独家报告，让您回顾过去的市场分析与研究员观点。

<!--locale:en-->

As part of your membership benefits, you have full access to both the latest and past articles and reports that are exclusive to paid members in the [Blog](https://en.macromicro.me/blog) section.

There are two main types of content:

1. 
**WEFC (Weekly Economic and Financial Commentary) :** a weekly summary highlighting 3–6 key topics based on recent macro and market developments, with concise, data-driven insights. You can also download the PDF version.

2. 
**Special Topic Reports :** published irregularly (about 6–8 articles per month), providing thematic analysis reports based on data, events, or market changes of major economies, helping you rationally understand important market dynamics starting from the data in real time.
