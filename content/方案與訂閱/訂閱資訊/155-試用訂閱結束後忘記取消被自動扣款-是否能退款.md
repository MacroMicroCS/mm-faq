原则上，体验方案未取消，则视为有意愿继续使用，而扣款后即开始使用付费方案，故无法提供已使用期间的退款，敬请见谅。
您可以随时取消自动续订，取消后，自下期起停止自动续约，目前效期内的使用权益不受影响，您仍可正常使用至效期结束。

取消订阅操作方式如下：
■ 信用卡付款订阅
前往「会员档案」→「[付费纪录](https://sc.macromicro.me/user/order-history)」，调整订阅状态即可。建议在扣款日前完成操作。

■ PayPal 订阅
请回到 PayPal 平台自行取消绑定。若操作遇到困难，也欢迎来信 support@macromicro.me，我们可以协助您处理。

📌 预防小提醒：体验方案期间，可随时至「付费记录」确认订阅状态、提前关闭自动续约。
⚠️ 请注意：持续订阅可终身保有加入时的优惠方案；取消后重新订阅将适用当时最新价格，供您参考。

As a general policy, not cancelling a trial is treated as consent to continue, and since the paid plan begins upon charging, refunds for time already used are not available — we appreciate your understanding.

You can cancel auto-renewal at any time. Cancellation stops the next cycle's renewal while your current access continues through the end of your paid period.

To cancel:
■ Credit card subscription
Go to Member Profile → [Payment History](https://en.macromicro.me/user/order-history) and adjust your subscription status. Please do this before your next billing date.

■ PayPal subscription
Cancel the recurring payment in your PayPal account. If you need assistance, email support@macromicro.me.

📌 Prevention tip: During your trial, you can check your subscription status and disable auto-renewal in Payment History at any time.
⚠️ Reminder: Continuous subscribers keep their original pricing. Cancelling and re-subscribing means current pricing applies.
