目前 1 枚 M幣 相當於 20 元新台幣，可以兌換：
・總經[線上學院](https://www.macromicro.me/video)的課程
・MM研究員撰寫的投資月報
・MM Prime 訂閱效期

獲取方式有兩種：
1. 透過信用卡直接購買 M幣
2. 參加[用戶觀點](https://www.macromicro.me/shared_chart)投票或透過[推薦碼](https://www.macromicro.me/user/mm-benefits)邀請好友訂閱，免費獲得 M幣

前往[我的M幣](https://www.macromicro.me/user/mcoins)即可查看目前持有數量和兌換選項。

M-Coins are MacroMicro's virtual currency, redeemable for services and products on the platform!

1 M-Coin is equivalent to NT$20 and can be used for:
・[Online Academy](https://en.macromicro.me/video) courses
・MM monthly investment reports
・MM Prime subscription time

There are two ways to earn M-Coins:
1. Purchase directly with a credit card
2. Earn for free by participating in [User Charts](https://en.macromicro.me/shared_chart) sentiment votes or referring friends

[Visit My M-Coins](https://en.macromicro.me/user/mcoins) to check your balance and explore redemption options.
