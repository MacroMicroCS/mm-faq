您的專屬[推薦碼](https://www.macromicro.me/user/mm-benefits)就在[會員檔案](https://www.macromicro.me/user/settings)裡，找起來很簡單！

登入訂閱帳號後，點擊網站右上角的[會員頭像選單](https://www.macromicro.me/user/settings)，就可以看到您的專屬訂閱推薦碼。

用推薦碼邀請好友訂閱後，雙方都可以獲得 M幣 作為回饋，歡迎多多分享！

<!--locale:zh-cn-->

您的专属[推荐码](https://sc.macromicro.me/user/mm-benefits)就在[会员档案](https://sc.macromicro.me/user/settings)里，找起来很简单！

登录订阅账号后，点击网站右上角的[会员头像菜单](https://sc.macromicro.me/user/settings)，就可以看到您的专属订阅推荐码。

用推荐码邀请好友订阅后，双方都可以获得 M幣 作为回馈，欢迎多多分享！

<!--locale:en-->

Your referral code is in your Member Profile — easy to find!

After logging in, click the member avatar menu in the upper-right corner →[Member Profile](https://en.macromicro.me/user/settings) and you'll see your personal referral code.

When friends subscribe using your code, both of you will earn M-Coins as a reward. Share away!
