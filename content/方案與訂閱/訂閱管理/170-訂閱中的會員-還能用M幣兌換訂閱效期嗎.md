  en: Can I redeem M-Coins to extend my subscription while already subscribed?

可以的！就算目前已有信用卡訂閱，仍然可以使用 M幣 來兌換新效期。
兌換完成後，您的訂閱效期將自動延長，信用卡的下次扣款日期也會隨之往後順延，非常方便！

前往「[我的M幣](https://www.macromicro.me/user/mcoins)」頁面 即可查看持有數量與兌換選項。

Yes! Even if you have an active credit card subscription, you can still use M-Coins to add more subscription time.

After redeeming, your subscription end date will be automatically extended, and your next credit card billing date will be pushed back accordingly.

Visit [My M-Coins](https://en.macromicro.me/user/mcoins) to check your balance and redemption options.
