[MM獨家報告](https://www.macromicro.me/mails/monthly_report) 屬於 [訂閱會員](https://www.macromicro.me/subscribe#individual)（MM PRO、MM Prime、MM Max、MM Business）的專屬權益，訂閱即可自動獲得觀看權限！

分為兩大類內容：

\n1. \n**投資月報** 每月發布，是最核心的定期報告，涵蓋全球總經分析與資產配置觀點。
\n2. \n**獨家快報** 不定期發布（約每月 6–8 篇），根據重要經濟體的數據、事件或行情變化，提供主題分析報告，即時帶您從數據出發理性看懂重要市場動態。
\n

**從數據出發，理性判斷**

總經分析報告之所以關鍵，在於它不只影響股市大盤，更會影響你投資的產業、個股。報告中，我們會從美國、中國、日本、歐洲、台灣、新興市場等主要經濟體下手分析，接著細談其對於股、匯、債、原物料等關鍵資產影響。

每一則獨家報告的收尾，還會給出「MM研究員」的獨家觀察，與針對資產的配置建議，幫助您做出務實且有效的投資決策，一起為自己的投資負責，掌握全球財富重分配的時刻！

[MM独家报告](https://sc.macromicro.me/mails/monthly_report) 属于 [订阅会员](https://sc.macromicro.me/subscribe)（MM PRO、MM Prime、MM Max、MM Business）的专属权益，订阅即可自动获得观看权限！

分为两大类内容：

\n1. \n**投资月报** 每月发布，是最核心的定期报告，涵盖全球总经分析与资产配置观点。
\n2. \n**独家快报** 不定期发布（约每月 6–8 篇），根据重要经济体的数据、事件或行情变化，提供主题分析报告，即时带您从数据出发理性看懂重要市场动态。
\n

**从数据出发，理性判断**

宏观分析报告之所以关键，在于它不只影响股市大盘，更会影响你投资的产业、个股。报告中，我们会从美国、中国、日本、欧洲、台湾、新兴市场等主要经济体下手分析，接着细谈其对于股、汇、债、原物料等关键资产影响。

每一则独家报告的收尾，还会给出「MM研究员」的独家观察，与针对资产的配置建议，帮助您做出务实且有效的投资决策，一起为自己的投资负责，掌握全球财富重分配的时刻！

[MM Exclusive Reports](https://en.macromicro.me/blog) are exclusive benefits for MM subscription members (MM PRO, MM Prime, MM Max, MM Business). Subscribing automatically grants you access to view them!

There are two main types of content:

\n1. \n**WEFC (Weekly Economic and Financial Commentary) :** a weekly summary highlighting 3–6 key topics based on recent macro and market developments, with concise, data-driven insights. You can also download the PDF version.
\n2. \n**Special Topic Reports :** published irregularly (about 6–8 articles per month), providing thematic analysis reports based on data, events, or market changes of major economies, helping you rationally understand important market dynamics starting from the data in real time.
\n

Besides [Exclusive Reports](https://en.macromicro.me/blog) in the blog, you can also find exclusive reports and analyses under [MM Daily](https://support.macromicro.me/hc/en-001/articles/8676449226639-What-is-the-MM-Daily-section-about) and [Spotlight](https://en.macromicro.me/spotlights) sections:

\n- \n[MM Daily](https://support.macromicro.me/hc/en-001/articles/8676449226639-What-is-the-MM-Daily-section-about)：With exclusive economic reports and research team's insight that are based on data-assisted research, addressing topics relevant to the world's major economies.
\n- \n[Spotlight](https://en.macromicro.me/spotlights): Highlighting a crucial chart or indicator for the week.
\n

**Empowering Independent Thinking**

MM investment reports feature briefings on significant events and data analysis, integrating perspectives from MM Analysts to assist in cultivating your investment acumen and fostering independent thinking.
