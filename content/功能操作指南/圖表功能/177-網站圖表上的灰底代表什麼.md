The gray shaded areas represent periods of US economic recession, marked by MacroMicro based on official determinations from the National Bureau of Economic Research (NBER) — the world's most authoritative institution on business cycle dating. For more information, please [see here](https://www.nber.org/research/business-cycle-dating).

💡 You can click the「Custom」button in the upper-right corner of any chart to show or hide the gray shading.

Want to add your own custom shading? This is a feature available to MM Prime and above (MM Prime, MM Max, MM Business). After subscribing, you can add shading for any time period in the [Research Toolbox](https://en.macromicro.me/toolbox/chart-builder/line) to create personalized charts.
