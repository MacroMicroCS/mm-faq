<!--locale:zh-cn-->

数据下载功能目前提供在 [MM Business 企业方案](https://sc.macromicro.me/subscribe#enterprise) 中，个人订阅方案（MM Prime、MM Max）不包含此功能。\
\
若您需要下载原始数据，欢迎前往[方案介绍](https://sc.macromicro.me/subscribe)页面了解 MM Business 的完整内容，或来信 business@macromicro.me，我们会有专人为您说明最适合的方案！

<!--locale:en-->

Data download is currently available under the [MM Business enterprise plan](https://en.macromicro.me/subscribe#enterprise). Individual subscription plans (MM Prime, MM Max) do not include this feature.\
\
If you need access to raw data downloads, visit [the plan page](https://en.macromicro.me/subscribe) to learn about MM Business, or email business@macromicro.me — our team will help you find the right fit.
