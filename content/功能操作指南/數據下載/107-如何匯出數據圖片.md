[成為 MM Prime 或 MM Business 訂閱會員](https://www.macromicro.me/subscribe) 可以在數據圖表右上方點選「匯出」下載數據圖表，也能於工具箱製圖並執行圖片匯出。MM Business 訂閱用戶可進一步去除浮水印，圖表使用更靈活。![截圖 2024-10-21 下午3.39.54.png](https://support.macromicro.me/hc/article_attachments/11032368439695)

<!--locale:zh-cn-->

[成为 MM Prime 或 MM Business 订阅会员](https://sc.macromicro.me/subscribe) 可以于数据图表右上方点选「汇出」下载数据图表，也能于工具箱制图并执行图片汇出。MM Business 订阅用户可进一步去除浮水印，图表使用更灵活。

\n

![](https://support.macromicro.me/hc/article_attachments/11032439187727)

<!--locale:en-->

[By becoming an MM Prime or MM Business subscriber](https://en.macromicro.me/subscribe), you can download charts by clicking on the "Export" button on the top right of each chart, and also use the "Toolbox" to customize and create charts for export. MM Business subscribers can further remove watermarks, making chart usage more flexible.
