MM Business 的團隊協作功能，可以讓多位成員共用下載額度和分享圖表，非常適合團隊使用！\
\
設定方式：\
點擊網站右上角的大頭貼 →「會員檔案」→「[團隊帳號管理](https://www.macromicro.me/user/subaccounts)」→ 新增協作者帳號，即可啟用協作功能。\
\
啟用後，團隊成員可共用數據下載額度（月繳每月 200 筆、年繳每月 500 筆），也可在「[我的儀表板](https://www.macromicro.me/user/dashboards)」中分享圖組及圖表，還能查看所有成員的[下載紀錄](https://www.macromicro.me/user/download-history)。\
\
若在設定過程有任何問題，歡迎來信 business@macromicro.me，我們很樂意協助！

<!--locale:zh-cn-->

MM Business 的团队协作功能，可以让多位成员共用下载额度和分享图表，非常适合团队使用！\
\
设置方式：\
点击网站右上角的头像 →「[会员档案](https://sc.macromicro.me/user/settings)」→「[团队帐号管理](https://sc.macromicro.me/user/subaccounts)」→ 新增协作者账号，即可启用协作功能。\
\
启用后，团队成员可共用数据下载额度（月缴每月 200 笔、年缴每月 500 笔），也可在「[我的仪表板](https://sc.macromicro.me/user/dashboards)」中分享图组及图表，还能查看所有成员的[下载记录](https://sc.macromicro.me/user/download-history)。\
\
若在设置过程有任何问题，欢迎来信 business@macromicro.me，我们很乐意协助！

<!--locale:en-->

MM Business's team collaboration features let multiple members share download quota and charts — ideal for teams!\
\
Setup:\
Click the avatar in the upper-right corner →「[Member Profile](https://en.macromicro.me/user/settings)」→「[Team Account Management](https://en.macromicro.me/user/subaccounts)」→ add a collaborator account to enable collaboration.\
\
Once enabled, team members can share the data download quota (200/month on monthly billing, 500/month on annual), share chart groups and charts in「[My Dashboard](https://en.macromicro.me/user/dashboards)」, and view all members' [download history](https://en.macromicro.me/user/download-history).\
\
If you run into any issues during setup, please email business@macromicro.me — we're happy to help!
