[數據下載是 MM Business 企業方案](https://www.macromicro.me/subscribe#enterprise)的專屬功能。若您已是 MM Business 訂閱者，卻無法下載，通常有以下幾種原因，我們來幫您一一確認：

1. 尚未同意數據使用協議：下載前需勾選同意 M平方數據使用協議規範，請確認是否已完成此步驟。

2. 下載額度已用完：MM Business 月繳方案每月共用 200 筆、年繳方案每月共用 500 筆，若已達上限，需等待次月重置或加購額度。

3. 該數據有版權限制：部分數據與數據夥伴合作，依合約範圍可能無法提供下載，這類數據在頁面上通常會有說明。

若您尚未訂閱 MM Business，歡迎前往 [方案介紹頁面](https://www.macromicro.me/subscribe)了解，或來信 business@macromicro.me，我們很樂意協助！

若已訂閱但確認以上原因後仍無法解決，歡迎來信 support@macromicro.me，我們會進一步協助您！

[数据下载是 MM Business 企业方案](https://sc.macromicro.me/subscribe#enterprise)的专属功能。若您已是 MM Business 订阅者，却无法下载，通常有以下几种原因，我们来帮您一一确认：

1. 尚未同意数据使用协议：下载前需勾选同意 M平方数据使用协议规范，请确认是否已完成此步骤。

2. 下载额度已用完：MM Business 月缴方案每月共用 200 笔、年缴方案每月共用 500 笔，若已达上限，需等待次月重置或加购额度。

3. 该数据有版权限制：部分数据与数据合作伙伴合作，依合约范围可能无法提供下载，这类数据在页面上通常会有说明。

若您尚未订阅 MM Business，欢迎前往 [方案介绍页面](https://sc.macromicro.me/subscribe)了解，或来信 business@macromicro.me，我们很乐意协助！

若已订阅但确认以上原因后仍无法解决，欢迎来信 support@macromicro.me，我们会进一步协助您！

<!--locale:en-->

[Data download is an exclusive feature of the MM Business enterprise plan](https://en.macromicro.me/subscribe#enterprise). If you're already an MM Business subscriber but can't download, here are the most common reasons:

1. You haven't agreed to the Data Usage Agreement: You must check the box to agree to MacroMicro's data usage terms before downloading. Please verify this step is complete.

2. Your download quota is used up: MM Business monthly plan shares 200 downloads/month; annual plan shares 500/month. If you've hit the limit, wait for the next monthly reset or purchase additional quota.

3. The data has copyright restrictions: Some data is sourced from partners and may not be available for download under their agreements. These datasets are usually noted on the page.

If you haven't subscribed to MM Business yet, [visit the plan page](https://en.macromicro.me/subscribe) or contact business@macromicro.me — we're happy to help!

If you're already subscribed and none of the above applies, please email support@macromicro.me and we'll investigate further.
