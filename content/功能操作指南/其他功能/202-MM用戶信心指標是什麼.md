MM用户信心指标是财经M平方自行设计的独家情绪指标，让社群用户的集体判断也能成为参考依据！\
\
每周透过投票方式，预测美国、中国、台湾、黄金和原油未来三个月的基本面走势，投票选项分为乐观、中立、悲观，从图表趋势可观察 MM 用户对近期基本面的整体信心程度。\
\
前往 [用户观点](https://sc.macromicro.me/shared_chart) 页面参与每周投票！

<!--locale:en-->

The MM User Sentiment Index is a proprietary sentiment indicator designed by MacroMicro, turning the collective judgment of our community into a reference signal!\
\
Each week, users vote to predict the 3-month fundamental outlook for the US, China, Taiwan, Gold, and Crude Oil. Options are Bullish, Neutral, or Bearish. The chart trend reflects the overall community confidence level on recent fundamentals.\
\
Visit the [User Charts](https://en.macromicro.me/shared_chart) page to participate in the weekly vote!
