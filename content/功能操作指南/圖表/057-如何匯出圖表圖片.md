成為 MM Prime/Max 或 MM Business 訂閱會員後，就可以匯出圖表圖片了！

操作方式：在圖表右上方點選[匯出](https://www.macromicro.me/toolbox/chart-builder/line)中自行調整製作圖，再匯出自訂版本。

💡 MM Business 訂閱用戶可以進一步去除浮水印，讓圖表在報告中使用更靈活！

<!--locale:zh-cn-->

成为 MM Prime/Max 或 MM Business 订阅会员后，就可以导出图表图片了！

操作方式：在图表右上方点选[导出](https://sc.macromicro.me/toolbox/chart-builder/line)中自行调整制作图，再导出自定义版本。

💡 MM Business 订阅用户可以进一步去除浮水印，让图表在报告中使用更灵活！

<!--locale:en-->

You can export chart images once you're an MM Prime/Max or MM Business subscriber!

[Click the「Export」button in the upper-right corner of any chart to download it. You can also build a customized chart in the Research Toolbox](https://en.macromicro.me/toolbox/chart-builder/line) and export your own version.

💡 MM Business subscribers can also remove the watermark for more flexible use in reports!
