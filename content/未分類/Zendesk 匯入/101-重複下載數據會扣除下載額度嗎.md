數據下載功能屬於 [MM Business 企業方案](https://www.macromicro.me/subscribe#enterprise)，下載額度由整個團隊共享。好消息是，重複下載同一份數據不會重複扣除額度！\
\
同一團隊成員在 1 小時內重新下載相同數據，系統提供免費重新下載，不會再扣除您的下載額度，請放心使用。\
\
您可以在數據[下載紀錄](https://www.macromicro.me/user/download-history)頁面查看所有下載歷史。

<!--locale:zh-cn-->

数据下载功能属于 [MM Business 企业方案](https://sc.macromicro.me/subscribe#enterprise)，下载额度由整个团队共享。好消息是，重复下载同一份数据不会重复扣除额度！\
\
同一团队成员在 1 小时内重新下载相同数据，系统提供免费重新下载，不会再扣除您的下载额度，请放心使用。\
\
您可以在数据[下载记录](https://sc.macromicro.me/user/download-history)页面查看所有下载历史。

<!--locale:en-->

Data download is part of the [MM Business enterprise plan](https://en.macromicro.me/subscribe#enterprise), with quota shared across the team. Good news — re-downloading the same dataset won't deduct your quota again!\
\
If a team member re-downloads the same data within 1 hour, the system allows a free re-download without deducting from your quota.\
\
You can review all download history on the [Download History](https://en.macromicro.me/user/download-history) page.
