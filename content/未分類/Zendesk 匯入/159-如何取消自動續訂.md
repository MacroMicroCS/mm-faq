### 信用卡付款訂閱

可自行於扣款日前到 [會員檔案](https://www.macromicro.me/user/settings) > [付費紀錄](https://www.macromicro.me/user/order-history) 調整訂閱狀態。建議在扣款日前完成操作。

### PayPal 訂閱

請回到 PayPal 平台取消綁定。

取消後，自下期起停止自動續約，目前效期內的使用權益不受影響，您仍可正常使用至效期結束。\
\
若有任何問題，歡迎隨時聯繫 support@macromicro.me！

<!--locale:zh-cn-->

您可以随时取消自动续订，操作方式如下：\
\
■ 信用卡付款订阅\
前往 [会员档案](https://en.macromicro.me/user/settings) > [付费纪录](https://sc.macromicro.me/user/order-history)，调整订阅状态即可。建议在扣款日前完成操作。\
\
■ PayPal 订阅\
请回到 PayPal 平台自行取消绑定。\
取消后，自下期起停止自动续约，目前效期内的使用权益不受影响，您仍可正常使用至效期结束。

\
若有任何问题，欢迎随时联系 support@macromicro.me！

<!--locale:en-->

You can turn off auto-renewal at any time:\
\
■ Credit card subscription\
Go to [Member Profile](https://en.macromicro.me/user/settings) > [Payment History](https://en.macromicro.me/user/order-history) and adjust your subscription status. Please do this before your next billing date.\
\
■ PayPal subscription\
Cancel the recurring payment directly in your PayPal account. If you need help, email support@macromicro.me.\
\
After cancellation, auto-renewal stops from the next billing cycle. Your current access continues unaffected until the end of your paid period.
