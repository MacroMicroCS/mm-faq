取消電子報訂閱有兩種方式：\
\
1. 直接在電子報中取消：每封電子報下方都有「cancel here」取消連結，點選即可退訂。\
\
2. 從會員設定取消：

\n- \n

  登入訂閱帳號後，點擊網站右上角的「會員頭像選單」→「[會員檔案](https://www.macromicro.me/user/settings)」

  \n

  ![](https://support.macromicro.me/hc/article_attachments/15517928366607)

  \n\n
\n- \n

  點擊「編輯」，即可修改電子報通知信箱設定等資料。

  \n

  ![](https://support.macromicro.me/hc/article_attachments/15517923811215)

  \n

  ![](https://support.macromicro.me/hc/article_attachments/15517923811471)

取消电子报订阅有两种方式：\
\
1. 直接在电子报中取消：每封电子报下方都有「cancel here」取消链接，点选即可退订。\
\
2. 从会员设置取消：

\n- \n

  登录订阅账号后，点击网站右上角的「会员头像菜单」→「[会员档案](https://sc.macromicro.me/user/settings)」

  \n

  ![](https://support.macromicro.me/hc/article_attachments/15517945090959)

  \n\n
\n- \n

  点击「编辑」，即可更改电子报收件邮箱。

  \n

  ![](https://support.macromicro.me/hc/article_attachments/15517940249999)

  \n

  ![](https://support.macromicro.me/hc/article_attachments/15517945092623)

  \n

There are two ways to unsubscribe from our newsletter:\
\
1. Unsubscribe directly from the email: Every newsletter has a「cancel here」unsubscribe link at the bottom — just click it.\
\
2. Unsubscribe from account settings: 

\n- \n

  First, logging in to your account and click the member avatar menu →「[Member Profile](https://en.macromicro.me/user/settings)

  ![]()

  \n\n
\n- \n
