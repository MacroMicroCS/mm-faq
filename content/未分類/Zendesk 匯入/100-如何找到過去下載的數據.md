數據下載功能屬於 [MM Business 企業方案](https://www.macromicro.me/subscribe#enterprise)。若您是 MM Business 訂閱者，可以這樣找到過去的下載紀錄：\
\
點擊網站右上角的「會員頭像選單」→ 前往「[數據下載紀錄](https://www.macromicro.me/user/download-history)」，即可查看近 30 天的所有下載紀錄。\
\
若需要查詢 30 天以前的紀錄，請來信 support@macromicro.me，我們可以協助確認。

数据下载功能属于 [MM Business 企业方案](https://sc.macromicro.me/subscribe#enterprise)。若您是 MM Business 订阅者，可以这样找到过去的下载记录：\

点击网站右上角的「会员头像菜单」→ 前往「[数据下载记录](https://sc.macromicro.me/user/download-history)」，即可查看近 30 天的所有下载记录。\
\
若需要查询 30 天以前的记录，请来信 support@macromicro.me，我们可以协助确认。

<!--locale:en-->

Data download is part of the [MM Business enterprise plan](https://en.macromicro.me/subscribe#enterprise). As an MM Business subscriber, here's how to find your download history:\
\
Click the member avatar menu in the upper-right corner → go to「[Download History](https://en.macromicro.me/user/download-history)」to view all downloads from the past 30 days.\
\
For records older than 30 days, please email support@macromicro.me and we can help.
