在 [總經](https://www.macromicro.me/macro) 單元，財經Ｍ平方由全球與主要經濟體切入，掌握整體趨勢；在 [產業](https://www.macromicro.me/industry-intelligence-hub/artificial-intelligence?sort=1&tag=377&chartPremiss=0,1&page=1) 單元，沿著產業鏈深入，結合供應鏈數據與個股財報，解析總經環境與產業如何交互影響。

而 [圖表 - 操盤人必看](https://www.macromicro.me/trader-insights)  則從標的與主題出發，由 MacroMicro 研究團隊從市場標的角度精選核心圖組，彙整包含股匯債原物料等各標的最值得關注的市場關鍵指標，包含 MM 獨家編制的基本面指數，適合需要快速掌握市場脈動的投資人每日瀏覽。

在[宏观](https://sc.macromicro.me/macro)单元，财经Ｍ平方由全球与主要经济体切入，掌握整体趋势；在 [产业](https://sc.macromicro.me/industry-intelligence-hub/artificial-intelligence?sort=1&tag=377&chartPremiss=0,1&page=1) 单元，沿着产业链深入，结合供应链数据与个股财报，解析总经环境与产业如何交互影响。

而 [图表 - 操盘人必看](https://sc.macromicro.me/trader-insights)  则从标的与主题出发，由 MacroMicro 研究团队从市场标的角度精选核心图组，汇整包含股汇债原物料等各标的最值得关注的市场关键指标，包含 MM 独家编制的基本面指数，适合需要快速掌握市场脉动的投资人每日浏览。

In the [Macro](https://en.macromicro.me/macro), MacroMicro approaches from a global and major economy perspective to grasp overall trends; in the [Sector](https://en.macromicro.me/industry-intelligence-hub/artificial-intelligence?sort=1&tag=377&chartPremiss=0,1&page=1), it delves deeply along the industry chain, combining supply chain data and individual stock financial reports to analyze how the macroeconomic environment and industries interact and influence each other.

[The Charts- Trader's Insight](https://en.macromicro.me/trader-insights)  starts from targets and themes, the MacroMicro research team curates a core set of charts from a market-asset perspective, covering key market indicators across equities, FX, bonds, and commodities — including MM Fundamental Index. Ideal for investors who need a quick daily pulse check on market conditions.
