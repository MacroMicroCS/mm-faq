數據下載功能是 [MM Business 企業方案](https://www.macromicro.me/subscribe#enterprise) 的專屬功能，下載額度由整個團隊共享（月繳每月 200 筆、年繳每月 500 筆）。\
\
如果當月額度已用完，有兩個方式可以繼續下載：\
\
1. 等待次月重置：每月月初額度會自動補滿，稍作等待即可繼續使用。\
\
2. 加購額外額度：如有緊急需求，歡迎來信 business@macromicro.me 詢問加購，我們會有專人為您說明。

<!--locale:zh-cn-->

数据下载功能是 [MM Business 企业方案](https://sc.macromicro.me/subscribe#enterprise) 的专属功能，下载额度由整个团队共享（月缴每月 200 笔、年缴每月 500 笔）。\
\
如果当月额度已用完，有两个方式可以继续下载：\
\
1. 等待次月重置：每月月初额度会自动补满，稍作等待即可继续使用。\
\
2. 加购额外额度：如有紧急需求，欢迎来信 business@macromicro.me 询问加购，我们会有专人为您说明。

<!--locale:en-->

Data download is an exclusive feature of the [MM Business enterprise plan](https://en.macromicro.me/subscribe#enterprise), with quota shared across the entire team (200 downloads/month on monthly billing, 500/month on annual billing).\
\
If your quota runs out, there are two options:\
\
1. Wait for the next monthly reset: Quota automatically refills at the start of each month.\
\
2. Purchase additional quota: If you need more urgently, contact business@macromicro.me and our team will assist you.
