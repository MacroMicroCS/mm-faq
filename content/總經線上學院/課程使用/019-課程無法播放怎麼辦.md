[總經線上學院](https://www.macromicro.me/video)的付費課程或展望無法播放有幾個常見原因：

1. 網路限制：課程使用 Vimeo 或 YouTube 服務，若您在印尼、中國等網路管制地區，需先透過 VPN 排除限制，一般瀏覽器（Chrome、Safari）即可正常觀看。

2. 廣告攔截外掛：部分 Ad Block 外掛可能阻擋播放功能，建議暫時關閉外掛後再試。

3. 確認網路連線：請確認您的網路設備連線正常後重新整理頁面。

若以上方式都確認過仍無法播放，歡迎來信 support@macromicro.me，並提供您的裝置資訊與錯誤截圖，我們會進一步協助您！

[线上学院](https://sc.macromicro.me/video)的付费课程或展望无法播放有几个常见原因：

1. 网络限制：课程使用 Vimeo 或 YouTube 服务，若您在印度尼西亚、中国等网络管制地区，需先通过 VPN 排除限制，一般浏览器（Chrome、Safari）即可正常观看。

[There are a few common reasons why paid courses or events in the Online Academy](https://en.macromicro.me/video) might not play:

1. Network restrictions: Courses use Vimeo or YouTube. If you're in a region with internet restrictions (e.g., Indonesia, China), you may need to use a VPN first. Standard browsers like Chrome or Safari work fine otherwise.

2. Ad-blocker extensions: Some ad-blocking plugins can interfere with playback. Try temporarily disabling the extension.

3. Check your internet connection: Make sure your network is stable, then refresh the page.

If none of the above resolves the issue, please email support@macromicro.me with your device info and a screenshot of the error — we'll help from there!
