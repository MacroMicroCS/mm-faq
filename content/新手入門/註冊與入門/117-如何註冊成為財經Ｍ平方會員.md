註冊財經M平方會員的步驟很簡單，以下是完整說明：

**註冊步驟**

\n1. 前往 [財經M平方](https://www.macromicro.me) 官網 <https://www.macromicro.me>\n
\n2. 點擊右上角的「**登入**」按鈕進去 [登入/註冊頁](https://www.macromicro.me/login?next=%2F)\n
\n3. \n

   註冊帳號分為以下兩大方式

   \n

   (1) 社群帳號：如果您尚未曾註冊成為一般會員，在直接選擇要連動社群帳號（用 Google / Facebook / WeChat ）並根據系統步驟執行之後，就會完成註冊；如果您已註冊過，則點擊社群帳號後會引導您登入。

   \n

   ![](https://support.macromicro.me/hc/article_attachments/15517018002575)

   \n

   (2) Email 帳號：請依照指引完成填寫，按下註冊後請前往信箱收取帳號開通信，即可建立新帳號。

   \n

   ![](https://support.macromicro.me/hc/article_attachments/15517018002703)

   \n
\n

**註冊後可以做什麼？**

成為一般會員（免費）後，你可以在限定範圍內瀏覽圖表與總經數據，是認識平台功能的好起點。如果之後有更多需求，例如查看獨家報告、使用研究工具箱、或進行量化回測，可以再考慮升級至付費方案。

如果想進一步了解ＭＭ目前提供的訂閱方案及功能差異，可以前往 [訂閱方案介紹](https://www.macromicro.me/subscribe) 查看！

Registering for a MacroMicro account is simple. Please follow the steps below:

**Registration Steps**

\n1. Go to the [MacroMicro website:](https://en.macromicro.me/) <https://en.macromicro.me/>
\n2. Click the “Log In” button at the top right corner to enter [the login/registration page](https://en.macromicro.me/login?next=%2F)
\n3. \n

   There are two ways to register:

   \n

   (1) **Social Account**:\
   If you have not previously registered, simply choose to sign up using a social account (Google / Facebook / WeChat) and follow the on-screen instructions to complete the registration.\
   If you have already registered, clicking a social account will guide you to log in.

   \n

   ![](https://support.macromicro.me/hc/article_attachments/15517179115791)

   \n

   (2) **Email Account**:\
   Please follow the instructions to fill in the required information. After clicking  Sign Up , go to your email inbox to receive the account verification email and complete the account setup.

   \n

   ![](https://support.macromicro.me/hc/article_attachments/15517241504655)

   \n
\n

**What can you do after registering?**

After becoming a free member, you can browse charts and macroeconomic data within a limited range, which is a great starting point to explore the platform.

If you need more advanced features—such as accessing exclusive reports, using the research toolbox, or conducting quantitative backtesting—you may consider upgrading to a paid subscription.

To learn more about MacroMicro’s subscription plans and feature differences, please visit [the Subscription Plans page.](https://en.macromicro.me/subscribe#individual)
