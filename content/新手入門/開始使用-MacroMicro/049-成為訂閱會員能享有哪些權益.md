成為 MM 訂閱會員後，可以享有豐富的數據與研究資源！以下是各方案的核心權益說明：

■ MM PRO
・總經圖表無限瀏覽
[・每月 6-8 篇獨家報告](https://www.macromicro.me/mails/monthly_report)+ 投資月報
・無廣告體驗
[・儀表板無限收藏](https://www.macromicro.me/user/dashboards)

■ MM Prime（含所有 PRO 功能）
[・研究工具箱](https://www.macromicro.me/toolbox/chart-builder/line)（20 種運算機制、8 種分析圖規格）
・量化策略回測
[・ETF 比較器與個股反查](https://www.macromicro.me/etf)
・圖表客製化匯出

■ MM Max（含所有 Prime 功能）
[・產業決策平台](https://www.macromicro.me/industry-intelligence-hub)
[・美股財報資料庫](https://www.macromicro.me/stocks/screener)
・個股篩選比較器
・電話會議逐字稿 AI 摘要

■ MM Business（含所有 Max 功能）
・原始數據下載（月繳每月 200 筆 / 年繳每月 500 筆，團隊共享）
・API 串接
・4 組團隊帳號管理
・圖表商業授權使用

各方案詳細內容與定價，請前往 [訂閱方案介紹頁面](https://www.macromicro.me/subscribe)查看。

As an MM subscriber, you unlock a wealth of data and research resources! Here's a breakdown of each plan's core benefits:

■ MM PRO
・Unlimited macro chart access
[・6–8 exclusive reports per month](https://en.macromicro.me/mails/monthly_report) + monthly investment report
・Ad-free experience
[・Unlimited dashboard saves](https://en.macromicro.me/user/dashboards)

■ MM Prime (includes all PRO features)
[・Research Toolbox](https://en.macromicro.me/toolbox/chart-builder/line) (20 calculation types, 8 chart formats)
・Quantitative strategy backtesting
[・ETF comparator and stock look-up](https://en.macromicro.me/etf)
・Custom chart export

■ MM Max (includes all Prime features)
[・Industry Intelligence Hub](https://en.macromicro.me/industry-intelligence-hub)
[・US Earnings Database](https://en.macromicro.me/stocks/screener)
・Stock screener and comparator
・Earnings call transcript AI summary

■ MM Business (includes all Max features)
・Raw data download (200/month on monthly billing, 500/month on annual billing — shared across team)
・API access
・4-account team management
・Commercial licensing for chart usage

For full details and pricing, [visit the subscription page](https://en.macromicro.me/subscribe).
